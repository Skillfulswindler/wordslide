A word game with a clock made of falling letters

Letters tumble down the mountainside and land in your tray. New ones shove the old ones along, and anything pushed off the end is gone for good. Build words on a 15×15 board — full crossword rules, real bonus squares — and clear the level before the slide buries you.

Seven worlds

Mudslide, Landslide, Avalanche, Volcano, Sandstorm, Waterfall, Blizzard — each one breaks the rules in its own way. Boulders land on the board. Embers burn letters out of your tray. Frost hides them.

Solo, against the clock

No opponent, no waiting for a turn. Just you, the tray, and the letters piling up.

A new board every day

The Daily gives everyone the same letters in the same order. Two minutes. Compare and share your score.

Pass-and-play duels

Same seed, same letters, two players, one phone. No account needed.
